# Privacy policy — ssoulss

Last updated: 15 December 2025

ssoulss (company) provides software solutions for healthcare and bookings. We operate in India and the United Kingdom and take privacy and data protection seriously.

Contact
- Email: info@ssoulss.com
- Data protection contact: dpo@ssoulss.com (or your nominated contact)

What this policy covers
- Personal data we collect (name, email, phone, appointment details, minimal patient metadata required for booking).
- Why we collect data: to provide services, support users and comply with legal obligations.
- Legal basis: contract, consent and legitimate interests where applicable. In the UK/EU we respect data subject rights under GDPR; in India we follow applicable data protection legislation and best practices.

Data processing & transfers
- Hosting: parts of the site are hosted on GitHub Pages; production services may be hosted with third-party cloud providers. We take reasonable safeguards for cross-border transfer.
- We do not sell personal data.

Retention
- Data is retained only as long as required for service operation, contracted retention and legal obligations. Contact us for specifics.

Your rights
- Access, rectification, deletion/erasure, portability, restriction and objection where applicable.
- To request deletion or data export, see our Delete Request page: /delete-request/

Security
- We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect data.

Children
- Our services are not for children under 16. If you believe a child’s data has been shared, contact us.

Changes
- We will update this page for material changes.

For legal advice specific to your requirements consult a qualified privacy lawyer. For privacy requests contact: info@ssoulss.com
