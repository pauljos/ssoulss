## Consulting Services

We help organisations design pragmatic AI strategies and deliver production-ready systems. Our consulting focuses on discovery, measurable pilots, and handover to internal teams.

{% endcapture %}

{% capture offerings %}

### Core services

- <strong style="color:#2b6fd6">Strategy & discovery</strong> — customer workshops, data readiness audits and opportunity prioritisation.
- <strong style="color:#f57c00">Proof-of-concept</strong> — rapid pilots to validate impact with minimal risk.
- <strong style="color:#43a047">Implementation</strong> — engineering, integrations, and MLOps to get models into production.
- <strong style="color:#6a1b9a">Training & handover</strong> — enable your teams to operate and iterate on delivered systems.

{% endcapture %}

{% capture process %}

### Our process

1. Align objectives and metrics
2. Explore data and architecture
3. Build a focused pilot
4. Validate and iterate
5. Deploy, monitor and handover

We emphasise short feedback loops and clearly defined success criteria.

{% endcapture %}
