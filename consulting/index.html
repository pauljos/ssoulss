## Consulting Services We help organisations design pragmatic AI strategies and deliver production-ready systems. Our consulting focuses on discovery, measurable pilots, and handover to internal teams.
### Core services - Strategy & discovery — customer workshops, data readiness audits and opportunity prioritisation. - Proof-of-concept — rapid pilots to validate impact with minimal risk. - Implementation — engineering, integrations, and MLOps to get models into production. - Training & handover — enable your teams to operate and iterate on delivered systems.
### Our process 1. Align objectives and metrics 2. Explore data and architecture 3. Build a focused pilot 4. Validate and iterate 5. Deploy, monitor and handover We emphasise short feedback loops and clearly defined success criteria.
### Case highlight We worked with a regional clinic network to reduce no-shows by 28% using predictive reminders and smarter scheduling rules. The pilot moved to production within 10 weeks and integrated with existing EHR systems.
